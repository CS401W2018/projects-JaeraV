What are different ways to style your form?

You can use the "fieldset" to but the form in a box and you can add background colors, and box shadows. Also to make the form look professional, you can put the different fields in a div tag to make it easier to align using either display grid or flex. You can also style input tags by using [type="submit"]and:not([type="submit"]). And you can make it responsive for mobile devices by adding a media query.

Why does the professor like to use flex?

Because it makes it easier to align and make the form look professional. It also prevents the children from overflowing in their alloted spaces.
