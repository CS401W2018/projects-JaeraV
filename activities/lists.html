What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL - Just like an unordered list but it is ranked.

UL - Unordered Lists, no ranking, order does not matter. It is just a list.

Sample:UL

Favorite Desserts

  • Brownies
  • Red Velvet Cupcake
  • Blueberry Cheesecake

Sample:OL

My Rankings

  1. Blueberry Cheesecake
  2. Brownies
  3. Red Velvet Cupcake
