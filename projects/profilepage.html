Hi! I am Jaera Villar

I was born and raised in the Philippines. Growing up, I can say that I had an amazing childhood experience. I spent a lot of my time playing outdoor games with my friends. We played 'patintero', 'tumbang preso', 'tagu-taguan', and many more. I was pretty active back then.

When my teen-age years came, I started to spend more time indoors. I am glad that there were resources available online in which we can enhance the skills we already have and discover or develop new ones. Through watching YouTube videos, I learned how to write simple calligraphy, paint using watercolor and acrylic, and even learned how to play few musical instruments.
