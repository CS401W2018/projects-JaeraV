Background

The table shows some of the Korean Albums that IZ*One released. IZ*One is a Korean - Japanese girl group formed through a survival show called Produce 48. The group was composed of nine South Korean members and three Japanese members. They had their debut on October 29, 2018. The group released songs and made promotions for two and a half years before they disbanded on April 29, 2021.
